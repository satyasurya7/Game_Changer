Problems in life and how Game Changer would help you deal with them.

It is very prudent to believe that life is a struggle, as the history explicitly had plethora of incidents which proves that mankind is suffering one or the other way in different times and different ages at different levels.

Especially in today’s times, where there’s discord in the world. Everything is uncertain, problems in life seems to be never ending & unresolving. Thus, individual feels, life is a struggle.

On the other hand, contradictory to the above, we also believe and Live our life’s in hope, that life in general is beautiful, excellence is the very nature of life. For the same we all are in search, to experience the beauty of life by attaining lasting Peace, Happiness and Success. Which is true, life in reality is beautiful but still none of us seems to be experiencing it. Besides, we often feel life is meaningless, at times we even question our existence in adversities, what is the purpose of our lives in this Physical World?

To understand the root cause of Peacelessness in the world today. Let’s go back in time, to have a fleeting glimpse of what were the biggest disasters in the history and it’s cause.

All the above are just the result of a weak mind, the inability to deal with life problems.

To sum up everything ‘’ Wars are born in the Minds of men and therefore defenses of peace should be constructed in the Minds of Men'' is a message in the charter of United Nations.

The message is very clear, the biggest of problems like world wars itself were originated from the Minds of Men. In addition, what else could be the reason for the problems in Individuals life? If not the Mind and it’s thinking disorder. And, it’s futile, without giving regard to the root cause, no matter how hard efforts we humans cast externally, it will not bring peace within oneself not in the world.

Therefore it’s essential to understand the effects of the Mind over Matter(Body). Mental health is an essential dimension of Health because the state of one’s Mind effects one’s physiological processes also. Good Health is most important for all Human tasks, devoid a moderate Health at least, individual is unable to perform Physical, Mental, Intellectual or Social tasks.

No matter how strong your body is. You can enjoy total health only if your mind is fine.

There has been a tremendous progress in the field of Medical Science. It has invented unprecedented equipment in diagnosing or treating Incurable Diseases. Performing great wonders by eradicating numerous Infectious Diseases. However, stress related epidemic rising proportionately. Hence, it is evident that deprive in Mental, Intellectual and Emotional Health in the Human Minds is giving rise to the diseases on the physical Level.

Therefore, in order to improve the physical health one should be giving more importance to Mind Health. Supporting the above, World Health Organization has defined Health, as a state of complete Physical, Mental and Social Well Being of a person and not merely the absence of Disease or Infirmity.

Einstein, the great scientist has observed Science can denature plutonium, but science cannot remove the evils in the hearts of Man. This observation reminds us that the evils and ill will in man needs to be removed with a spiritual hand.

W.H.O has defined attributes of physically healthy person. It has said that a physically healthy person has normal anatomical and physiological state of Cardiovascular, Digestive, Endocrine and Respiratory System etc. Also the person has bodily weight, respiration, blood pressure etc. Within the normal limits as per one’s age, gender and height.

In addition. A Socially healthy person fulfils his responsibilities towards his parents. He gives enough attention for proper development of children. He co-operates in planning the peaceful and happy family life. He makes friendship which is satisfying and lasting. He tries to prevent any loss being done to the society. He does spiritual service of his fellow beings to show them the means to peaceful and happy life. He tries to prevent the activities that adversely affect the society. Unfortunately, none of the above is done to the degree of excellence by any Individual in today’s world for the reason being Emotionally Weak.

A study done to assess the mental health William C. Menninger drew up the following questions:

Are you always worrying?
Are you unable to concentrate because of unrecognized reasons?
Are you continually unhappy with out justified cause?
Do you lose your temper easily and often?
Do you have wide fluctuations in your mood from depression to elation, back to depression, which incapacitate you?
Are you troubled by regular insomnia?

Do you continually dislike to be with people?
Are you upset if the routine of your life is disturbed?
Do your children consistently get on your nerves?
Are you browned off and constantly bitter?
Are you afraid without real cause?
Are you always right and other person always wrong?
Do you have numerous aches and pains for which no doctor can find a physical pulse?
One can judge from the above discussion and questions, whether He or She is Mentally Healthy?

If the answer is No. Then you have arrived at the right place.
